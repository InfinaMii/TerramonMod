In the Terramon mod, you can catch and evolve all of your favourite Pokemon from the Kanto region!
The mod also features:
- A new NPC who sells you important items such as Pokeballs
- Rare Candies dropped from bosses to level up and evolve your Pokemon
- A new Pokemon Trainer costume for you to wear
- A music box to listen to music with i guess
