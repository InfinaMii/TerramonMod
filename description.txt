The Terramon Mod is a cross between Terraria and the Pokemon franchise!
Currently, this mod features:
• Over 100 Pokemon!
• Refined Poke Ball mechanics
• A new NPC to buy Poke Balls from
• Rare Candies and Stones to evolve your Pokemon
• A Pokemon Trainer vanity outfit
• A new Pokemon Center music track
